We’re announcing a powerful new capability in the Azure Maps Visual for Power BI: Markers . This feature makes it easier than ever for organizations to create compelling, data-driven location stories—without the need for complex GIS tools. With Markers , you can: Use meaningful icons or custom images instead of basic shapes to represent deliveries, facilities, or assets—think trucks, warehouses, or alerts.
