Power BI’s latest update introduces an enhancement to how users interact with reports with the general availability of modern visual tooltips . All Power BI reports—from Power BI Desktop to Power BI reports in the web, in the mobile app, in Teams, and embedded in any website—now use the updated visual tooltips, making report interactions easier with the built-in actions footer and report creation faster with tooltip styling and colors coming from the report theme. By default, visual tooltips show the details of the visual's data point report consumers hover over, such as the name and value.
